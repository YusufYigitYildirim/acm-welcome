WELCOME TO ITU ACM!

ITU ACM Student Chapter was established under the supervision of Assoc. Prof. Dr. Berk Canberk within the Istanbul Technical University Computer and Informatics Faculty in September 2017. It aims to bring together students interested in Computer Engineering and Computer Science, fostering technical development through various project teams and study groups with the support of faculty members. Additionally, the chapter aims to enhance members' social skills such as teamwork and responsibility. ITU ACM Student Chapter continues its efforts to represent Istanbul Technical University successfully in national and international computer science and programming competitions organized by universities and companies.Some of our effective activities include hosting technical training series featuring proficient individuals and academics in computer sciences, representing our university and country in national and global competitions on computer sciences and algorithms, specialized advanced study groups within the club with faculty support (e.g., Algorithms, Data Structures, Android and iOS App Development), introductory level workshops aiding university programming courses (e.g., Introduction to C Programming, Python, JavaScript), and facilitating technical events related to IT and science with active participation from our members. Every year, we also host events like Local Hack Day and Google HashCode at ITU.
